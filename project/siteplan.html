Project Site Plan

Site Name: Flavor Vault

This name suggests a special place where users can save and explore flavor-packed recipes, highlighting the favorites functionality and unique cooking experience.

Site Purpose:

FlavorVault provides a curated platform for users to explore diverse recipes across multiple categories while allowing them to save their favorite dishes for easy access. The site will include detailed recipe instructions, ingredient lists, and a user-friendly interface with tools to personalize their cooking journey.

Scenarios:

        1- "How can I save my favorite recipes so I can easily find them later?"
        2- "Are there quick and easy dinner recipes for busy weeknights?"

Color Schema:

        1- Primary Color: #FF6F61 (Coral Red)

            ◾ Use: This vibrant color will be used for headings, buttons, and 
            call-to-action elements to grab attention and evoke warmth and 
            excitement related to cooking.

        2-  Secondary Color: #FFF4E6 (Soft Cream)

            ◾ Use: This soft, neutral color will serve as the background for the 
            site, creating a clean and inviting space that highlights the content 
            without overwhelming the user.

        3-  Accent Color: #4CAF50 (Fresh Green)

            ◾ Use: For icons, hyperlinks, and highlights, adding a fresh and 
            natural vibe that complements the food theme.

Typography:

        1- Primary Font: Poppins (Sans-serif)

            ◾ Use: For headings and navigation links. This font is modern, 
            clean, and highly readable, perfect for grabbing attention 
            while maintaining a professional look.

        2- Secondary Font: Roboto (Sans-serif)

            ◾ Use: For body text and paragraphs. Its simplicity and excellent 
            readability make it ideal for recipe instructions and detailed 
            content.

        3- Accent Font: Dancing Script (Cursive)

            ◾ Use: For decorative purposes, such as the site logo or 
            occasional callouts. Its playful and elegant style adds a 
            personal, culinary touch to the site.
